OUR DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS

Strategic programs improving the lives of people across Enugu State.

L-PRES Livestock Productivity & Resilience Support Project

Coordinator: Dr Ifeyinwa Elizabeth Nnajieze

This project strengthens livestock production, improves food security, and enhances rural livelihoods by supporting farmers with modern livestock management practices.

Nigeria For Women Programme

Coordinator: Barr. Mrs Blessing C. Ogbonna

Empowering women economically through cooperative groups, financial inclusion, and entrepreneurship development across communities in Enugu State.

RAAMP – Rural Access & Agricultural Marketing Project

Coordinator: Engr. Greg Nnaji

Improving rural road infrastructure to help farmers transport goods to markets efficiently and increase agricultural productivity.

IFAD / VCDP Value Chain Development Programme

Coordinator: Project Office

Enhancing agricultural value chains and supporting rural farmers with training, market access, and financial support.

NWWSRP National Urban Water Sector Reform Project

Coordinator: Engr. Eze Charles

Expanding urban water infrastructure to ensure reliable clean water supply across Enugu urban areas.

AGILE – Adolescent Girls Initiative for Learning & Empowerment

Coordinator: Project Office

Supporting adolescent girls with education access, skills development, and empowerment programs.

NEWMAP Climate Adaptation & Watershed Project

Coordinator: Pamela Nonyelum Ikpa

Addressing erosion and climate related environmental challenges through watershed management and infrastructure solutions.

FADAMA Agricultural Development Project

Coordinator: Geoffrey Ndubisi Eze

Providing agricultural support, irrigation systems, and rural development initiatives to improve farmers' productivity and income.
